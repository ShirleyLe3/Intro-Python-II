You can type commands to move your character
around the world and interact with your
environment.
Here is a list of commands you can type:

q                             | Quit game
------------------------------------------------------------
help                          | Display this help file
------------------------------------------------------------
n, e, s, w                    | Move in a cardinal direction
------------------------------------------------------------
look                          | Look at your surroundings
------------------------------------------------------------
take, get [item name]         | Take an item
------------------------------------------------------------
drop [item name]              | Drop an item
------------------------------------------------------------
inventory, i, bag             | List items in your bag
------------------------------------------------------------
inventory, i, bag [item name] | Inspect item in your bag
------------------------------------------------------------
